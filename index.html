About SHAPE

The importance of exposure

a 2-week program, commencing on the 24th of April and extending till the 5th of May 2023. We aim to give the students a thorough exposure to the Engineering branches through a hands-on training on interesting and doable projects in 10 days. The purpose of the program is to induce technical curiosity, and help them explore, analyse and come up with (possibly) out-of-the-box solutions to existing problems.

Departments @ SSN SSN Facilities

"an exciting opportunity for school students who have completed their 11th grade. This program intends to raise student awareness of the potential of the various engineering disciplines and help them make an informed choice of further study or career"

120+ Students will be sharing this opputunity

Our Departments

During the programme, the participants (interns) will be given an opportunity to visit all the engineering departments of SSNCE (Computer Science Engineering, Information Technology, Electricals and Communication Engineering ,Electricals Electronics Engineering, Mechanical, Civil, Chemical, Bio-Medical) ; during their visit the senior faculty and the students will help them have a basic understanding of that department. Each of the interns will be given an individual project, supported by a faculty for guidance.

Project Exhibition and Certification

Other than working on a technical project, the interns will also be exposed to SSN Library, Engineering Labs, Sports facilities and other facilities of the College. At the end of the program, the students will exhibit their work in a project exhibition. Best projects will be awarded. All Participants will receive a Certificate
